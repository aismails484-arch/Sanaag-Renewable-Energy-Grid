# Grid Management & Operational Protocols

## 1. Smart Load Balancing
* **Priority Routing:** The AI grid controller prioritizes critical loads (Port Cold Storage and Hospital Units) during low-sunlight hours.
* **Surplus Management:** Excess energy during peak sunlight (11 AM - 3 PM) is redirected to water desalination and pump storage.

## 2. Maintenance Schedules
* **Solar Units:** Bi-weekly cleaning of panels in high-dust plateau areas (Bancadde).
* **Wind Units:** Quarterly mechanical inspection of turbine bearings in coastal salt-spray zones (Laasqoray/Maydh).

## 3. Security & Resilience
* **Redundancy:** Modular grid design ensures that a failure in one solar farm does not impact the entire regional grid.
* **Cyber-Security:** Protecting the IoT grid-controllers from external interference via localized SPH Global encryption.
