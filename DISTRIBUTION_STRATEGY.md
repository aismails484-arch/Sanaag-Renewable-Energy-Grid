# Regional Grid Distribution & Transmission

## Phase 1: The Industrial Loop
Linking the **Bancadde Solar Hub** with the **Maydh/Xiis Port corridor**. This enables surplus energy from the plateau to power the coastal industrial facilities.

## Phase 2: Urban Modernization
Upgrading the existing Erigavo power infrastructure to integrate renewable sources, targeting a 70% reduction in electricity costs for households and small businesses.

## Phase 3: Rural Microgrids
Deploying containerized "Solar-in-a-Box" microgrids for remote pastoralist communities and Al-Madow highland villages not connected to the main grid.
