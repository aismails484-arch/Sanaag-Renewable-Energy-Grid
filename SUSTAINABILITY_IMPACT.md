# Sustainability & Environmental Impact Analysis

## 1. Carbon Emission Reduction
* **Target:** Replacing 85% of diesel-generated power in Sanaag with renewable energy.
* **CO2 Savings:** Estimated reduction of 12,000 tons of Carbon Dioxide (CO2) annually within the first 5 years of full operation.

## 2. Ecological Protection
* **Pollution Mitigation:** Eliminating soil and water contamination caused by fuel leaks from traditional diesel generators at ports and farms.
* **Noise Reduction:** Switching to silent solar and low-decibel wind turbines to protect the natural wildlife in the Al-Madow and Golis ranges.

## 3. Economic Sustainability (Carbon Credits)
* **Carbon Trading:** Positioning the SPH Global energy grid for international Carbon Credit certification, creating a secondary revenue stream for the project.
* **Resource Efficiency:** Using 100% solar energy for water desalination and irrigation, ensuring zero-waste operations.
