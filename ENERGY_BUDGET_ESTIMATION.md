## 2. Operational Expenditure (OPEX)
* **Maintenance:** $25,000 annually (Cleaning, sensor calibration, and grid repairs).
* **Personnel:** $15,000 annually for the local technical monitoring team.

## 3. Revenue & Impact Model
* **Industrial Tariffs:** Revenue generated from powering Port operations and Mechanized Farms.
* **Payback Period:** Estimated at 4.5 years based on current diesel-to-renewable cost savings.
* **Subsidized Energy:** Providing low-cost electricity to 5,000+ households in Sanaag.
