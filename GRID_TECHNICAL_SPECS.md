# SREG Technical Specifications & Engineering

## 1. Solar PV Component
* **Technology:** N-Type Monocrystalline Bifacial modules (high-efficiency).
* **Inverters:** Multi-MPPT Industrial Hybrid Inverters (100kW+ units).
* **Mounting:** Reinforced anti-corrosion tracking systems to maximize energy yield.

## 2. Wind Energy Component
* **Turbines:** Vertical Axis Wind Turbines (VAWT) for coastal deployment to handle varied wind directions.
* **Durability:** Marine-grade coating to prevent salt-spray corrosion.

## 3. Smart Storage (BESS)
* **Battery Tech:** LiFePO4 (Lithium Iron Phosphate) for safety and long cycle life.
* **Grid Controller:** AI-driven load balancing to prioritize critical infrastructure (Ports, Hospitals, Water Pumps).
