# Sanaag Renewable Energy Grid (SREG)

## 1. Executive Overview
The **Sanaag Renewable Energy Grid (SREG)** is a foundational infrastructure project designed to provide clean, affordable, and reliable power to the Sanaag region. By leveraging the region's high solar irradiance and strong coastal winds, SREG will power the agricultural mechanization of the plains and the industrial port operations of the coast.

## 2. Strategic Objectives
* **Energy Sovereignty:** Eliminating reliance on expensive and volatile fossil fuel imports.
* **Industrial Support:** Providing low-cost, 24/7 electricity for cold-storage (Fish/Produce) and heavy-duty irrigation pumps.
* **Sustainability:** Reducing carbon emissions while fostering economic growth through green technology.

## 3. Hybrid Energy Architecture
* **Coastal Wind Farms (Xiis - Laasqoray):** Harnessing consistent Gulf of Aden winds for industrial port usage.
* **Plateau Solar Hubs (Bancadde - Hadaaftimo):** Large-scale solar PV farms with automated tracking systems.
* **Smart Storage (BESS):** High-capacity Lithium-Ion Battery Energy Storage Systems to ensure grid stability and night-time availability.
